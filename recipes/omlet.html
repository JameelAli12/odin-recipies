Omlet

Description

This is a simple recipe made for the people who want to gain weight aka bulking.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbspn oil
  • 3 eggs
  • Spicies of your choice
  • Sauce

Steps

  1. Add 1 tbsp or more oil to the pan and let it heat.
  2. Crack open the eggs in a bowl and mix the spcies with the eggs.
  3. Add the mixed material to the pan and let it cook
  4. Check the bottom side of the omlet and flip if its cooked.
  5. Finally, check the softness of the omlet and serve hot.
