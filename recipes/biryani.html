Pasta

Description

This is a simple recipe made for the people who want to gain weight aka bulking.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbspn oil
  • 100 gm meat
  • 200 gm rice
  • Spicies of your choice
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Add 5 tbsp or more oil to the pan and let it heat.
  2. Add spicies of your choice e.g. onion, garlic, etc and cook them until soft.
  3. Add meat and mix it well with the spicies added.
  4. Cove pan and let it heat at lower flame for some minutes.
  5. Check the softness of the meat
  6. Finally, add rice to the pan and mix it well with the meat and let it steam for some minutes and serve hot.
